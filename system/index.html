RALLY Hybrid Movement System

Movement codes

DB Clean Step-Up

The goal is not to win every workout. The goal is to keep more useful capacity for more years.

Operating model

Separate the pieces before you raise the dose.

Do something daily, but separate stress. Build aerobic, strength, power, movement, and recovery reserves with scalable dose rules.

daily not maximal

Daily Does Not Mean Maximal

Daily training works when low days stay low and high days are deliberately concentrated.

four reserves

Build Four Reserves

The longevity athlete needs cardiorespiratory reserve, strength reserve, power reserve, and movement reserve.

clean dose

Clean Dose Before More Complexity

Every prescription should define intent, floor, target, and cap before adding exercises.

scale identity

Scale Dose, Preserve Identity

When readiness is low, reduce load, volume, density, range, or complexity before changing the session intent.

Reserve stack

Build useful capacity, not just workout tolerance.

Movement identity, dose, day type, and scaling tier stay separate so daily training does not become daily high stress.

Use 2-4 per week depending on athlete level and recovery evidence.

Medium Or Threshold Day

Use deliberately; do not let medium become the default.

controlled threshold · tempo · local muscular endurance · density

Readiness

Scale the dose before changing identity.

green

Run target plan.

sleep normal · warm-up normal · motivation normal · no pain escalation

yellow

Keep session identity, reduce volume 20-40%, avoid failure.

poor sleep · bar speed down · RPE high · unusual soreness · HR elevated

red

Switch to restore day or seek qualified clinical input when appropriate.

pain escalation · illness signs · major performance drop · REDs warning signs · severe fatigue
